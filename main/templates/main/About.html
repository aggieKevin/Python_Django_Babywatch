Frequently Asked Questions

What is Babywatch?

BabyWatch is a website that allows you to search for playdates. It was developed by four students from Mays Business School, Texas A&M University. Their vision is to help families connect with each other while they get their kids to socialize.

Is this service paid?

Yes, absolutely! We do not charge a dime from the parents.

Why should I undergo a Background check for using a website?

This website allows parents to enter their kids information such as name, age, gender, etc. and even upload photographs. Also, all users are going to set up playdates for their kids using our service. While we strive to ensure that this data is secured, we also want to ensure that no user with a criminal background gets to access the website. This is why we do a background check on all our users to ensure the safety of families using our service.

How will I know that the Background check is completed?

You will receive an email from us with the results of your check. If your check is successful, you will then be able to login and enjoy full functionalities of our website.

I cannot find my Zip Code. What should I do?

We are greatful for your interest in our community. BabyWatch is currently only available for Brazos County, Texas. We are in the process of expanding and hope to extend our service to all counties in Texas soon.
